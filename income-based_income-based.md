# Income-Based Credits
## Description
Credits targeted at low- and moderate-income working taxpayers, where the amount depends primarily on earned income and family size rather than on a specific expense.
## Credits in this folder
- Earned Income Credit (earned-income-credit.md) — Schedule EIC → Form 1040, Line 27. Fully refundable
## Notes
- The Earned Income Credit is one of the largest refundable credits, claimed on about 17.2 million returns (IRS SOI, TY 2022)
- The Saver's Credit is also income-based but is filed under retirement-savings/
## Questions
- Did you work and have relatively modest income?
- Do you have qualifying children?
## Prompt
- Do I qualify for the earned income credit?
- I work but do not earn much. What credits are there?
