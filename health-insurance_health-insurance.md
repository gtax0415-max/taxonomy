# Health Insurance Credits
## Description
Credits that subsidize health coverage bought through the Health Insurance Marketplace.
## Credits in this folder
- Premium Tax Credit (premium-tax-credit.md) — Form 8962 → Schedule 3, Line 9 → Form 1040, Line 31. Refundable
## What changed for 2026
The ENHANCED premium tax credit created in 2021 EXPIRED at the end of 2025. Beginning in 2026 the credit reverts to its original pre-2021 form: the 400%-of-federal-poverty-level income cliff returns, subsidies are smaller, and excess advance payments must be repaid in full with no repayment caps. This is one of the largest practical changes for 2026 filers.
