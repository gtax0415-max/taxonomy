# Form 1099-DIV\
## Description\
Dividend income reporting document from stocks and mutual funds.\
## Required Information\
- Payer name\
- Payer TIN\
- Ordinary dividends (Box 1a)\
- Qualified dividends (Box 1b)\
- Federal withholding\
## Questions\
- Did you receive dividends from stocks or mutual funds?\
- Were any of your dividends qualified dividends?\
## Common Errors\
- Missing 1099-DIV\
- Confusing ordinary and qualified dividends\
- Forgetting Schedule B when dividends exceed $1,500\
## Prompt\
- I received a 1099-DIV for dividends from my stock investments.\
- My mutual fund paid me dividends this year.\
- I got dividend income from several companies I own shares in.\
- I have qualified dividends reported on my 1099-DIV.}
