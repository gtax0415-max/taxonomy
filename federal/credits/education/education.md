# Education Credits
## Description
Credits for qualified higher-education expenses, claimed on Form 8863 (about 8.7 million returns, IRS SOI TY 2022).
## Credits in this folder
- American Opportunity Credit and Lifetime Learning Credit (education-credits.md) — Form 8863. The AOTC is 40% refundable and lands on Form 1040 Line 29; the nonrefundable parts go through Schedule 3, Line 3 to Form 1040 Line 20
## Related, but not credits
- The student loan interest DEDUCTION is a deduction, not a credit — see federal/deductions/adjustments/student-loan-interest.md
- Scholarship and fellowship grants not reported on Form W-2 are INCOME on Schedule 1, Line 8r
## Questions
- Did you, your spouse, or a dependent pay tuition and fees?
- Did you receive Form 1098-T from the school?
- Is the student in the first four years of a degree program?
## Prompt
- I paid college tuition this year.
- My son is in college and I want to claim an education credit.
