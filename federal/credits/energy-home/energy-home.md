# Energy and Home Credits
## Description
Credits for residential energy improvements and clean vehicles. IMPORTANT: the One Big Beautiful Bill Act TERMINATED most of these credits years ahead of their original schedule. For tax year 2026, the major residential energy credits no longer exist.
## Termination dates set by OBBBA
| Credit | Code section | Last available |
|---|---|---|
| Energy Efficient Home Improvement | 25C | property placed in service through Dec 31, 2025 |
| Residential Clean Energy (solar, battery, geothermal) | 25D | expenditures through Dec 31, 2025 |
| New Clean Vehicle (EV) | 30D | vehicles acquired through Sep 30, 2025 |
| Previously Owned Clean Vehicle (used EV) | 25E | vehicles acquired through Sep 30, 2025 |
| Alternative Fuel Vehicle Refueling Property (EV charger) | 30C | property placed in service through Jun 30, 2026 |
| New Energy Efficient Home (builders) | 45L | homes acquired through Jun 30, 2026 |
## What this means practically
- A solar installation, heat pump, or insulation job COMPLETED in 2026 or later gets NO federal credit. The date that matters is when the property was placed in service, not when it was ordered or paid for
- Work finished by December 31, 2025 can still be claimed on the 2025 return using Form 5695
- The EV charger credit (30C) and the builder credit (45L) are the only ones with any 2026 window, and both close June 30, 2026
- State and utility incentives are unaffected by OBBBA and may still apply

## Questions
- Was the energy property PLACED IN SERVICE on or before December 31, 2025?
- Did you buy an electric vehicle on or before September 30, 2025?
- Are you installing an EV charger before June 30, 2026?
- Have you checked state and utility incentives, which are unaffected?
