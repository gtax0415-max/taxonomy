# Foreign Tax Credits
## Description
Credit for income tax paid or accrued to a foreign country or U.S. possession, designed to prevent the same income being taxed twice.
## Credits in this folder
- Foreign Tax Credit (foreign-tax-credit.md) — Form 1116 → Schedule 3, Line 1 → Form 1040, Line 20
## The most common case is simpler than it looks
Many taxpayers encounter foreign tax only as a small amount withheld on international mutual funds or ADRs, shown in Form 1099-DIV Box 7. Below a modest threshold, the credit can be claimed DIRECTLY on Schedule 3 without filing Form 1116 at all.
## Questions
- Did you pay foreign income tax, or have it withheld?
- Does your Form 1099-DIV show an amount in Box 7?
- Do you have foreign wages, rental income, or a foreign business?
## Prompt
- I paid taxes to another country.
- My 1099-DIV shows foreign tax paid.
- I worked abroad this year.
