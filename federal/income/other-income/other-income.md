# Other Income
## Description
Income that does not fit the main categories and is generally reported on Schedule 1, Part I, then carried to Form 1040, Line 8. Individual sources are detailed in the files below.
## Forms and sources
- Form W-2G — Gambling and lottery winnings (w-2g.md)
- Alimony received — Depends on the divorce date (alimony.md)
## Notes
- Most of these flow through Schedule 1, Part I to Form 1040, Line 8
- All are taxable income even when no form is issued
## Questions
- Did you have income that does not fit wages, interest, dividends, capital gains, retirement, or business?
- Did you win money gambling or receive alimony?
## Prompt
- I have some other income that does not fit the usual categories.
- I won money and received a form for it.
- I receive alimony from a former spouse.
