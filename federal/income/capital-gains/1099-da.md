# Form 1099-DA
## Description
Digital asset proceeds from broker transactions, used to report sales, exchanges, or other dispositions of cryptocurrency, stablecoins, and NFTs.
## Required Information
- Broker name
- Broker TIN
- Description of digital asset
- Date acquired and date sold
- Proceeds
- Cost basis (may not be provided for 2025 transactions)
## Questions
- Did you sell, exchange, or dispose of any digital assets through a broker this year?
- Do you know your cost basis for each transaction?
- Did you use more than one exchange or platform?
## Common Errors
- Missing cost basis (brokers may not report it for 2025)
- Failing to report transactions when no 1099-DA was received
- Confusing proceeds with taxable gain
## Prompt
- I sold some cryptocurrency this year and received a 1099-DA from my exchange.
- My crypto broker sent me a 1099-DA for my Bitcoin trades.
- I traded digital assets on more than one platform, so I have several 1099-DA forms.
- I disposed of some NFTs and got a 1099-DA.
