# Farm Income
## Description
Income from operating a farm — raising crops, livestock, poultry, fish, or fruit. Reported on Schedule F, which flows through Schedule 1 Line 6 to Form 1040 Line 8. About 1.84 million returns file Schedule F (IRS SOI, TY 2022).
## Forms in this folder
- Schedule F — Profit or Loss From Farming (farm-income.md)
## Notes
- Farming is a trade or business, so net profit is subject to SELF-EMPLOYMENT TAX and Schedule F net profit also flows to Schedule SE
- Farm income is QBI-eligible — see federal/deductions/qbi/qbi.md
- Renting farmland for a share of the crop without materially participating goes on Form 4835 or Schedule E instead of Schedule F
- Selling farm ASSETS (machinery, breeding livestock, land) is reported on Form 4797, not Schedule F — see federal/income/capital-gains/sale-of-business-property.md
## Questions
- Do you operate a farm, ranch, or similar agricultural business?
- Do you materially participate, or do you rent the land out?
- Did you sell farm equipment, breeding livestock, or land this year?
## Prompt
- I run a farm and need to report my income.
- I have income from raising crops and livestock.
