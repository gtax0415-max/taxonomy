# Form 1099-DIV — Box 5: Section 199A Dividends
## Description
The portion of Box 1a that may be eligible for the 20% qualified business income (QBI) deduction under Section 199A. Typically from REIT dividends.
## Notes
- Included in Box 1a
- Flows through the QBI deduction (Form 8995 / 8995-A)
- Common if you hold REITs or REIT-containing funds
## Common Errors
- Missing the QBI deduction on REIT dividends
## Prompt
- My 1099-DIV shows Section 199A dividends in Box 5.
- I have REIT dividends that qualify for the QBI deduction.
