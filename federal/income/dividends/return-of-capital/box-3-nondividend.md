# Form 1099-DIV — Box 3: Nondividend Distributions
## Description
A return of your original investment (basis) rather than a distribution of earnings. Not taxable when received, but it lowers your cost basis.
## Notes
- Reduces basis in the investment
- Amounts received in excess of basis become taxable capital gain
## Common Errors
- Reporting it as taxable dividend income
- Failing to track the basis reduction for future sales
## Prompt
- My 1099-DIV shows a nondividend distribution in Box 3.
- I received a return of capital this year.
