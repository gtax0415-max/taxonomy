# Form 1099-DIV — Box 2a: Total Capital Gain Distributions
## Description
Capital gain distributions passed through from mutual funds or REITs. Treated as long-term capital gains regardless of how long you held the fund.
## Notes
- Reported on Schedule D
- Comes from funds/ETFs, not individual stocks
## Common Errors
- Treating these as ordinary dividends
- Forgetting to carry them to Schedule D
## Prompt
- My mutual fund reported capital gain distributions on my 1099-DIV.
- I have capital gain distributions in Box 2a.
