# Form 1099-DIV — Box 1a: Total Ordinary Dividends
## Description
Total ordinary dividends received during the year, including reinvested dividends. This is the full pie; Box 1b is a slice of it.
## Notes
- Taxed at ordinary income rates
- Includes the amount shown in Box 1b (do not add them together)
## Common Errors
- Double-counting Box 1a and Box 1b
- Forgetting Schedule B when dividends exceed $1,500
## Prompt
- I received dividends from my stock investments this year.
- My brokerage reported total ordinary dividends on my 1099-DIV.
