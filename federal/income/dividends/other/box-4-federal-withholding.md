# Form 1099-DIV — Box 4: Federal Income Tax Withheld
## Description
Federal income tax withheld from your dividends (backup withholding). Counts toward your total tax payments.
## Notes
- Relatively uncommon
- Credited against total tax owed
## Common Errors
- Forgetting to claim the withholding as a payment
## Prompt
- Federal tax was withheld from my dividends.
- My 1099-DIV shows backup withholding in Box 4.
