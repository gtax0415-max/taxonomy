# Form 1099-DIV — Box 1b: Qualified Dividends
## Description
The portion of Box 1a that qualifies for lower long-term capital gains tax rates.
## Notes
- Already included in Box 1a
- Taxed at 0%, 15%, or 20% depending on income
- Requires a holding-period test to qualify
## Common Errors
- Adding Box 1b on top of Box 1a (double-counting)
## Prompt
- Some of my dividends are qualified dividends.
- My 1099-DIV shows qualified dividends eligible for lower rates.
