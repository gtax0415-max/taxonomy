# Form 1040 — Line 1a: W-2 Wages
## Description
Total wages, salaries, and tips from Box 1 of every Form W-2 you received. This is the main wage line and the largest component of Line 1 for most employees.
## Notes
- Box 1 wages are often less than gross salary because of pre-tax items like 401(k) contributions and cafeteria-plan (health insurance) deductions
- Add together Box 1 from all W-2s if you had more than one employer
- Earned income; subject to Social Security and Medicare tax withheld by the employer
## Required Information
- Employer name
- EIN
- Wages (Box 1)
- Federal income tax withheld (Box 2)
## Questions
- Did you receive one or more W-2 forms?
- Did you have more than one employer during the year?
## Common Errors
- Missing a W-2 from a short-term or former employer
- Using gross salary instead of the Box 1 amount
- Wrong SSN on the form
## Prompt
- I have been employed and received a Form W-2.
- I received W-2s from more than one employer this year.
- My W-2 shows my wages in Box 1 and federal tax withheld in Box 2.
