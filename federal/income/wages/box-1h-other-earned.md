# Form 1040 — Line 1h: Other Earned Income
## Description
Earned income that does not fit any other Line 1 sub-line and was not reported on a W-2 — a catch-all for wage-type income earned as an employee, such as certain cash wages with no W-2.
## Notes
- This is EMPLOYEE / wage-type earned income, which is why it belongs on Line 1 rather than Line 8 (self-employment)
- If the work was actually self-employment (working for yourself), the income belongs on Schedule C → Line 8 instead
- The distinction is whether you were someone's employee or in business for yourself
## Required Information
- Description (type) of the income
- Amount
## Questions
- Did you earn wage-type income that was not on a W-2 and does not fit the other Line 1 sub-lines?
- Were you an employee for this work, or were you self-employed?
## Common Errors
- Putting self-employment income here instead of on Schedule C / Line 8
- Leaving off cash wages because no form was issued
## Prompt
- I earned some wage income that was not reported on a W-2.
- I have other earned income that does not fit anywhere else.
