# Form 1099-INT
## Description
Interest income reporting document from banks and financial institutions. Individual boxes are detailed in the files below.
## Boxes
- Box 1 — Interest income → Form 1040, Line 2b (taxable/)
- Box 3 — U.S. savings bonds and Treasury interest → Form 1040, Line 2b, state-exempt (taxable/)
- Box 8 — Tax-exempt interest → Form 1040, Line 2a (tax-exempt/)
- Box 9 — Specified private activity bond interest → Form 1040, Line 2a + Form 6251 AMT (tax-exempt/)
## Questions
- Did you receive interest from a bank or financial institution?
- Was your total taxable interest over $1,500?
- Did you have any tax-exempt or municipal bond interest?
## Prompt
- I received a 1099-INT from my bank.
- My 1099-INT has several boxes filled in, including tax-exempt interest.
