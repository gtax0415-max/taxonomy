# Form 1099-INT
## Description
Interest income reporting document from banks and financial institutions.
## Required Information
- Payer name
- Payer TIN
- Interest income
- Federal withholding
## Questions
- Did you receive interest from a bank or financial institution?
- Was your total interest income over $1,500?
## Common Errors
- Missing 1099-INT
- Forgetting Schedule B when interest exceeds $1,500
## Prompt
- I received a 1099-INT from my bank for the interest on my savings account.
- My bank paid me interest this year and sent me a form.
- I have interest income from a certificate of deposit.
- I earned interest from more than one bank, so I have several 1099-INT forms.
