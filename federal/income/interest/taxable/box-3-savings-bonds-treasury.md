# Form 1099-INT — Box 3: Interest on U.S. Savings Bonds and Treasury Obligations
## Description
Interest earned on U.S. savings bonds and Treasury notes, bills, and bonds.
## Notes
- Taxable at the federal level (reported on Form 1040, Line 2b)
- Exempt from state and local income tax
- Some savings bond interest may be excludable under the education savings bond exclusion (see Pub. 550)
## Common Errors
- Reporting it on the state return (it is state-tax-exempt)
- Missing the education savings bond exclusion when eligible
## Prompt
- I have interest from U.S. Treasury bonds on my 1099-INT.
- My 1099-INT shows savings bond interest in Box 3.
- I earned interest on Treasury bills this year.
