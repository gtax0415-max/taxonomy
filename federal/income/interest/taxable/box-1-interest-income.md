# Form 1099-INT — Box 1: Interest Income
## Description
Ordinary taxable interest, such as interest from bank savings accounts, CDs, and corporate bonds.
## Notes
- Taxed at ordinary income rates at both federal and state level
- Requires Schedule B if total taxable interest exceeds $1,500
## Common Errors
- Missing 1099-INT
- Forgetting Schedule B when interest exceeds $1,500
## Prompt
- I received a 1099-INT from my bank for interest on my savings account.
- My bank paid me interest this year.
- I earned interest from a certificate of deposit.
