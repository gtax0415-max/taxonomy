# Form 1099-INT — Box 8: Tax-Exempt Interest
## Description
Tax-exempt interest, typically from municipal bonds issued by state and local governments.
## Notes
- Not subject to federal income tax
- Reported on Form 1040, Line 2a for informational purposes
- May be exempt from state tax if the bond was issued in your state
- Can still affect other calculations (e.g., taxability of Social Security)
## Common Errors
- Failing to report it (still required for information, even though not taxed)
- Assuming it is exempt from your state tax regardless of the issuing state
## Prompt
- I have tax-exempt interest from municipal bonds on my 1099-INT.
- My 1099-INT shows tax-exempt interest in Box 8.
- I earned interest from muni bonds this year.
