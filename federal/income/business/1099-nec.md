# Form 1099-NEC
## Description
Nonemployee compensation reporting document for independent contractors and freelancers.
## Required Information
- Payer name
- Payer TIN
- Nonemployee compensation
- Federal withholding
## Questions
- Did you receive nonemployee compensation as an independent contractor?
- Did you have business expenses to deduct against this income?
## Common Errors
- Missing 1099-NEC
- Forgetting to deduct business expenses on Schedule C
- Not paying self-employment tax
## Prompt
- I did freelance work for XYZ Corporation and received a 1099-NEC.
- I'm an independent contractor and got a 1099-NEC for my services.
- I received nonemployee compensation for consulting work this year.
- I run a small business and several clients sent me 1099-NEC forms.
