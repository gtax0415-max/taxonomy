# Form 1099-MISC (business context)
## Description
Form 1099-MISC is documented in full under rental/, because since 2020 it mainly reports rents and royalties (Schedule E). This pointer covers the less common case where a 1099-MISC amount is actually business income.
## Which Schedule 1 line applies
The destination depends on whether the amount comes from a TRADE OR BUSINESS you operate:
- YES, it is business income → Schedule C, then Schedule 1 Line 3 (business income). Net profit is subject to SELF-EMPLOYMENT TAX and may qualify for QBI
- NO, it is a one-off prize or award → Schedule 1 Line 8i (prizes and awards). No self-employment tax
- NO, and it is not a prize → Schedule 1 Line 8z (other income), with a description
- It is rent or royalty → not this file; see rental/1099-misc.md, which routes through Schedule E to Schedule 1 Line 5
All of these paths total on Schedule 1 Line 10 and land on Form 1040 Line 8.
## Notes
- Nonemployee compensation for services now comes on Form 1099-NEC, not 1099-MISC — see 1099-nec.md
- The self-employment tax consequence is the practical reason the Schedule C versus Schedule 1 Line 8 distinction matters. Business income costs an extra 15.3%; a one-off prize does not
- Schedule 1 Line 8j exists for income from an activity NOT engaged in for profit (a hobby). Hobby income is reported there, and hobby expenses are not deductible
## Questions
- Is the Box 3 amount part of an ongoing trade or business you operate?
- Is it a one-time prize, award, or settlement?
- Do you have related expenses (deductible only if it is a business)?
## Common Errors
- Putting a one-off prize on Schedule C and paying unnecessary self-employment tax
- Putting genuine business income on Schedule 1 Line 8z and missing both the expense deductions and the QBI deduction
- Treating hobby income as a business
## Prompt
- I received a 1099-MISC that is actually from my business.
- I got a 1099-MISC and I am not sure if it is business or rental income.
- I won a prize and received a 1099-MISC with an amount in Box 3.
