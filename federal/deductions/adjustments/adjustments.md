# Adjustments to Income (Above-the-Line)
## Description
Deductions taken on Schedule 1, Part II that reduce total income to arrive at Adjusted Gross Income (AGI) on Form 1040 Line 11. Often called "above-the-line" because they come before the standard/itemized deduction choice.
## Why they matter
- Available whether you take the standard deduction OR itemize — you never have to give one up for the other
- They reduce AGI itself, and many other tax benefits (credits, phase-outs) are based on AGI, so these deductions can have a double benefit
