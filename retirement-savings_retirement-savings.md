# Retirement and Savings Credits
## Description
Credits that reward retirement saving by lower- and moderate-income taxpayers.
## Credits in this folder
- Saver's Credit, formally the Credit for Qualified Retirement Savings Contributions (savers-credit.md) — Form 8880 → Schedule 3, Line 4 → Form 1040, Line 20
## Notes
- Form 8880 appears on about 9.4 million returns (IRS SOI, TY 2022), making it one of the more common credits despite being poorly known
- The credit is IN ADDITION to any deduction for the contribution itself, so a traditional IRA contribution can produce both a deduction and a credit
## Questions
- Did you contribute to an IRA, 401(k), or similar plan?
- Is your income modest enough to qualify?
## Prompt
- I contributed to my 401(k). Is there a credit for that?
- I put money in an IRA this year.
